Yes/No Question

  • Is it the same for all Americans? Can it be simply stated?
  • Is it a myth?
  • Is it simply a quest for a better life?

Response Question

  1. How has the American Dream changed over time?
  2. Some see their dreams wither and die while others see their dreams fulfilled. Why?
  3. Everyone has dreams about a personally fulfilled life ...what is your dream?
  4. How do diverse cultures view the American Dream?
  5. What comes to mind when you consider the “American Dream?”
  6. Why do people from other countries immigrate to America?
  7. What are some of the obstacles to achieving the American Dream?
  8. Which groups of people have had trouble attaining “The American Dream”?
  9. Why do you think this is? Given the obstacles that some people have to overcome, what makes the American Dream appealing?
  10. What does “The American Dream” mean for you personally?
  11. How have significant historical events affected the American Dream?
  12. How will new opportunities in the 21st century challenge the American Dream? What is your American Dream?
  13. How will YOUR personal dream become a part of America's (and the world's) future?
